Why Fly Fishing

Fly fishing slows you down. You start to notice currents, hatches, shadows, and how wind touches the water. In eastern Idaho, that awareness comes with big skies, volcanic canyons, and clear, cold rivers shaped by snowmelt.

Purpose of Site

This site focuses specifically on eastern Idaho water and fish. Conditions change with season and flow, but the sense of peace is always there. I've kept things simple as I am fairly new to the region/sport.
